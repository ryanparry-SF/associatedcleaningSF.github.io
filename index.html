Associated Cleaning is ready to expand its Customer Community Plus portal from 70 to 470 members. This add-on locks in the same A$6.30/member/month rate, with a proposed start date of 1 July 2026 — aligning with the new financial year and co-terming to your current contract expiry of 29 January 2027.

Associated Cleaning currently has 70 registered portal members at A$6.30/member/month. This proposal adds 400 members at the same rate, bringing the total to 470 members. Associated Cleaning is committed to transacting within two weeks. Two paths are available: activate as an add-on from 1 Jul 2026 co-termed to 29 Jan 2027, or include within an early renewal for a clean multi-year agreement — see the Early Renewal Bundle tab.

Why Expand Your Member Allocation?

Self-Service at Scale

Clients log jobs, view compliance documents, track schedules, and raise requests — all without calling your support team. More members means more self-service, fewer inbound tickets.

Competitive Differentiation

An active, branded customer portal is increasingly a procurement requirement for enterprise FM contracts. Capacity demonstrates operational readiness to new and renewing clients.

Business Case

Ready to transact. Associated Cleaning has identified 400 new portal members and is committed to proceeding within two weeks. A 1 July 2026 start date aligns with the new financial year, ensures every new member is onboarded from day one of FY27, and can co-term cleanly with the existing contract.
Operational cost avoidance. Each new client handled purely via phone/email support rather than self-service portal consumes approximately 3–5 hours of admin time per month. 30 additional portal clients saves an estimated 90–150 hours/month.
Retention driver. Portal-engaged clients show significantly higher contract renewal rates in the facilities management sector. The portal surfaces value — job completion data, compliance records, SLA reporting — that reinforces the relationship at renewal time.
Per-member economics stay strong. At A$6.30/member/month, you're spending less per client on digital engagement than most organisations spend on a single inbound support interaction. Expanding volume doesn't change the unit economics — it amplifies them.

Proposal 2 · New Capability

A Branded Mobile App for
Your 470 Portal Members

Mobile Publisher turns your Customer Community Plus portal into a native iOS and Android app — published in the App Store and Google Play as "Associated Cleaning". Your portal members download your app, not Salesforce's. Built for customers, not internal employees.

Associated Cleaning currently has no branded mobile app for its Community portal members. Mobile Publisher changes that — packaging your existing Community portal pages and workflows into a native iOS and Android app for customers, with zero development required. The 470-user licence is sized to align precisely with your post-expansion Community Plus member count. This proposal is for customers only — the app serves as the mobile version of the community portal, not an internal tool for employees.

Key Capabilities

Your Brand in the App Store

"Associated Cleaning" appears in the Apple App Store and Google Play. Your portal members download and recognise your app — not Salesforce's. A direct trust signal with every enterprise client.

Native Mobile Community Portal

Your 470 Community Plus members access their portal through a native iOS and Android app. No browser, no generic login screen — a seamless branded experience purpose-built for your customers.

Push Notifications for Service Updates

Notify members when jobs are completed, compliance certificates are ready, service schedules are updated, or new reports are available — native push notifications reduce inbound enquiries.

Perfectly Aligned to Member Count

470 Mobile Publisher licences maps precisely to the post-expansion Community Plus member count. One product, one quantity — every portal member gets a native mobile experience, no allocation gaps.

Zero Development Required

Mobile Publisher packages your existing Community portal configuration — pages, flows, records — via Salesforce point-and-click. No iOS or Android developers needed. Live in 4–6 weeks.

Offline Access for Members

Customers can view service history, job records, and compliance documents even without connectivity. Data syncs automatically when they reconnect — no disruption to the member experience.

Why This Matters for Associated Cleaning

No current customer mobile app. Associated Cleaning has no branded mobile touchpoint for its Community portal members today. Mobile Publisher fills that gap — every portal member gets a native app experience without a single line of code being written.
A natural extension of Proposal 1. The 470-user quantity isn't arbitrary — it aligns exactly with the post-expansion Community Plus member count. Proposal 1 grows the portal to 470 members; Proposal 2 puts a branded native app in every one of those members' hands.
Customer experience as a differentiator. In commercial cleaning, digital maturity is increasingly a tender requirement. Clients who interact with Associated Cleaning via a dedicated branded app — not a browser or a phone call — perceive a higher standard of service and professionalism.
Better engagement than browser-based portal. Native apps drive significantly higher engagement than mobile browser sessions. Push notifications — only available through a native app — allow Associated Cleaning to proactively communicate service milestones without waiting for customers to log in.
